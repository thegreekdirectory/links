What We Do

Craftsmanship
You Can Feel.

From a minor fender bender to major collision damage, our shop has the skill, equipment, and over three decades of experience to restore your vehicle — often better than before the accident ever happened.

01

Collision & Accident Repair

Full structural and cosmetic restoration after accidents. We work directly with your insurance company and handle front-end, rear-end, and side-impact damage.

02

Paint & Finish Restoration

Color-matched paint work indistinguishable from the original factory finish. We obsess over the blend so you never notice where the repair ends.

03

Dent & Scratch Removal

Paintless dent repair and traditional bodywork to eliminate dings, creases, and scrapes. We also restore headlight covers to factory clarity — quickly and affordably.

04

Rust Repair & Prevention

Replacing rusted rockers, panels, and fenders before they compromise your car's structure. Done correctly the first time, with quality materials that last.

05

Bumper Repair & Replacement

We repair what can be saved and source quality replacements when needed. We'll even install a part you bring in — no hassle, honest pricing.

06

Insurance Claim Assistance

We've navigated hundreds of insurance claims. We'll document the damage, communicate with your insurer, and advocate for the repair quality your car deserves.

07

Auto Frame Testing

Precision frame measuring and alignment testing after any impact. Your safety depends on a straight frame — we verify it before returning your keys.

08

Steering & Suspension

Post-collision steering and suspension restoration ensuring your car doesn't just look straight — it drives straight, safely, from day one after pickup.

09

Routine Maintenance

General automotive maintenance to keep your vehicle in peak condition. A trusted shop that knows your car as well as you do — and treats it with the same care.

Our Story

More Than a
Body Shop.

Since 1989, Mark Lekas has been the quiet, trusted corner of Glenview where people bring their cars — and their stress — after an accident.

What makes Lekas different isn't just the quality of the work (though customers call it "better than new"). It's the honesty. The owner, Mark, is super honest and knowledgeable — you get straight answers, fair prices, and a shop that treats your car the way he'd treat his own.

From navigating insurance claims, to fixing a stranger's door free on the spot, to sourcing a used door to save a customer money — this is a shop built on doing the right thing, every time.
