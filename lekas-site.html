What We Do

Craftsmanship
You Can Feel.

From a minor fender bender to major collision damage, our shop has the skill, equipment, and decades of experience to restore your vehicle — often better than before it happened.

01

Collision & Accident Repair

Full structural and cosmetic restoration after accidents. We work directly with your insurance company to take the stress off you.

02

Paint & Finish Restoration

Color-matched paint work that's indistinguishable from the original factory finish. We obsess over the blend so you don't notice the repair.

03

Dent & Scratch Removal

Paintless dent repair and traditional bodywork to eliminate dings, creases, and scrapes — even the ones you think are "too small to fix."

04

Rust Repair & Prevention

Cutting out and replacing rusted panels before they compromise your car's structure. Done right the first time, with quality materials that last.

05

Bumper Repair & Replacement

Cracked, scratched, or completely destroyed bumpers — we repair what can be saved and source quality replacements when needed, at honest prices.

06

Insurance Claim Assistance

We've navigated hundreds of insurance claims. We'll help you understand your options, document the damage, and get what your car deserves.

Our Story

More Than a
Body Shop.

For over two decades, Mark and the Lekas team have been the quiet, trusted corner of Glenview where people bring their cars — and their stress — after an accident.

What makes Lekas different isn't just the quality of the work (though customers call it "better than new"). It's the honesty. You'll get a straight answer, a fair price, and a shop that treats your car the way they'd treat their own.

From navigating insurance claims for a family rear-ended by a drunk driver, to fixing a stranger's car door for free on the spot — this is a shop built on doing the right thing, every time.
