Our Story

From the Heart of Crete
to the Heart of Chicago

🇬🇷  Founded by John Brillakis · Xaniá, Crete, Greece

Brillakis Import Foods was born from a simple passion: bringing the authentic flavors of Greece to the Greek-American community — and to anyone who craves the real thing. John Brillakis, originally from the storied city of Xaniá on the island of Crete, built this store on a foundation of personal pride, deep product knowledge, and genuine hospitality.

Today, his son Manolis continues that tradition — greeting every customer with warmth, speaking knowledgeably about every bottle of tsipouro on the shelf, every wheel of authentic feta, and every jar of Cretan honey sourced directly from Greek producers.

We are more than a grocery store. We are a piece of Greece — a cultural gathering place where language, food, and community come together. Whether you're Greek and homesick for the pantry staples of your childhood, or simply curious about the Mediterranean's most celebrated cuisine, you belong here.

Our Selection

The Finest of Greece,
Under One Roof

Hand-selected imports from trusted Greek producers — from the olive groves of Crete to the distilleries of Macedonia.

🍶
Spirits & Liqueurs
Ouzo · Tsipouro · Raki · Metaxa
One of the most extensive Greek spirit collections in the greater Chicago area. Dozens of labels and brands, including rare Cretan raki and regional tsipouro varieties.
🫒
Olive Oil & Olives
Extra Virgin · Kalamata · Imported
Premium extra-virgin olive oils imported directly from Greece. Kalamata olives, green olives, and olive products you simply cannot find at a regular grocery store.
🧀
Greek Cheeses
Feta · Kefalograviera · Kasseri
Authentic imported feta cheese from Greek distributors — better quality and better priced than any supermarket. Sourced the right way, the Greek way.
🍯
Honey & Preserves
Cretan Honey · Thyme · Wildflower
Including the legendary thyme honey of Crete — widely regarded as among the finest in the world. Jams, preserves, and sweet Greek pantry staples.
🛒
Pantry & Dry Goods
Pasta · Legumes · Spices · Coffee
All the Greek pantry essentials: orzo, gigantes beans, dried herbs, mountain tea, Greek coffee, tahini, and much more — imported for authenticity.
📿
Cultural & Religious
Icons · Books · Souvenirs · Gifts
Religious articles, Greek Orthodox icons, Greek-language books, worry beads, evil eye charms, and unique souvenirs from Greece — a cultural treasure trove.
